One Piece Card

One Piece Card Game (ONE PIECEカードゲーム, Wan Pīsu Kādo Gēmu?) is a trading card game (TCG) released by Carddass and licensed by Shueisha. First announced on March 7, 2022, this card game released in Japan in July 2022 as part of One Piece's 25th-anniversary celebrations.[1] The game released worldwide later in 2022.[2]
